So happy that you want to get to know me!

My passion for design began at a young age, and was solidified when I studied Media and Communication in high school. It was there that I fell head over heels for Graphic Design.

After graduating from high school, I embarked on my Bachelor of Design journey at the University of Queensland. I'm currently in my second semester and have gained invaluable knowledge and skills.

My area of focus is Information Environments (IT), and I aspire to have a career that allows me to combine my technical and design expertise. As a hardworking, creative, and solution-oriented designer/developer, I am confident that I can bring immense value to any team.
